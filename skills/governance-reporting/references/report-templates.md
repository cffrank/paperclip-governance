# Governance Report Templates

Aligned with Holdings operating rhythm.

## Weekly Division Check-in Template

```markdown
## Weekly Check-in — Week of [DATE]

### Financial (from CFO)
- Total spend: $[X] / $[Y] monthly budget ([Z]%)
- Notable: [any flags or anomalies]

### Operations (from COO)
- Agents: [X] active, [Y] issues
- Tasks: [X] completed, [Y] in progress, [Z] blocked
- Notable: [any flags]

### Wins
- [achievement 1]
- [achievement 2]

### Blockers
- [blocker 1] — Owner: [agent], Action needed: [what]

### Key Metrics
| Metric | Target | Actual | Status |
|---|---|---|---|

### Next Week Priorities
1. [priority]
2. [priority]
3. [priority]
```

## Monthly Board Operating Review Template

```markdown
## Monthly Operating Review — [MONTH YYYY]

### 1. Financial Review (30 min)
[CFO financial report — budget vs actual, variances, recommendations]

### 2. Division Reports (45 min)

#### Operations
[COO health report — agent health, task velocity, stale work]

#### Compliance
[COO compliance report — findings, violations, remediation]

#### Risk
[COO risk report — register summary, new risks, mitigations]

### 3. Decisions This Month
| Date | Decision | Outcome | Follow-up |
|---|---|---|---|

### 4. Strategic Topic
**Question for next month:** [one strategic question for the human board to consider]

### Action Items
| Action | Owner | Due |
|---|---|---|
```

## Quarterly Business Review Template

```markdown
## Quarterly Business Review — Q[N] [YYYY]

### 1. Quarter Review
**Plan vs. Reality:**
| Goal | Target | Actual | Status |
|---|---|---|---|

### 2. Portfolio Review
| Project | Revenue/Value | Spend | Health | Recommendation |
|---|---|---|---|---|

### 3. Resource Allocation
- Current budget: $[X]/month
- Recommended changes: [rebalancing proposals]
- Hiring needs: [roles needed]

### 4. Risk Register Summary
| Risk | Score | Trend | Mitigation Status |
|---|---|---|---|

### 5. OKR Assessment
| OKR | Target | Progress | Status |
|---|---|---|---|

### 6. Next Quarter Plan
**Priorities:**
1. [priority with rationale]
2. [priority]
3. [priority]

**Budget:** $[X]/month (change: [+/-]%)
**Hires:** [planned hires]
```
